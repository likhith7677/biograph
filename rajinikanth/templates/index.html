Biography

Shivaji Rao Gaekwad, known as Rajinikanth, was born on December 12, 1950. From being a humble bus conductor in Bangalore, he rose to become India's biggest cinematic icon. His screen presence, punch dialogues, and humble nature made him a living legend.

Rajinikanth has acted in over 160 films across Tamil, Hindi, Telugu, and Kannada. His life is an inspiring story of determination and self-belief, making him a symbol of hope and charisma.
